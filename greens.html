Greens

About

At Green's Grill & Sushi Bar, we proudly blend the rich flavors of Mediterranean cuisine with the artistry of sushi, creating a unique culinary fusion. Our menu boasts delectable options like Hummus and Pita, Classic Uramaki, and enticing vegetarian dishes.

Menu

Greens offers fries either as a Basket of French Fries for $5.99 or Basket of Sweet Potato Fries for $5.99.

Style

Straight-cut

Texture

These fries have a good balance of a crispy exterior and a fluffy interior. They normally lean towards a fluffy look, but they feature a unique outer crisp that you won't find in any other fries on this list.

Flavor

These fries have basic seasonings, simply salt.

Dipping Sauce

Greens offers a basic dipping sauce option of ketchup.

Cost Value Analysis

$6 for a full basket is pretty good!

Variability Rate

33% - 3 visits and one bad experience

The one bad experience was due to getting them as take out. A lot of fries do not hold up well as take out so it's best to order and eat these fresh.

Overall

These fries have an enticing outer crisp layer that helped these fries make this magazine. Green's menu is mostly Mediterranean and sushi so you may not think to order fries, but I can guarantee these are worth it. They also have sweet potato fries if that's what you prefer. Order them for the table or as your side and you won't regret it!
